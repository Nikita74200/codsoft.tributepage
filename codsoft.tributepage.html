Tribute to Captain Vikram Batra

Remembering a True Hero

Captain Vikram Batra, PVC (9 September 1974 – 7 July 1999) was an officer of the Indian Army, posthumously awarded the Param Vir Chakra, India's highest and most prestigious award for valour, for his actions during the 1999 Kargil War.

Achievements

  • Participated in Operation Vijay during the Kargil War.
  • Captured Point 5140, a crucial peak, from the enemy.
  • Chanting "Yeh Dil Maange More" became a symbol of his determination.
  • Posthumously awarded the Param Vir Chakra for his extraordinary bravery.

In Tribute

Captain Vikram Batra's selfless courage, dedication, and sacrifice will always be remembered as an inspiration to all. He exemplified the true spirit of a soldier and his legacy lives on in the hearts of every Indian.
